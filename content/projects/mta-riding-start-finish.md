## Riding MTA Start to Finish

I absolutely love trains, especially the public infrastructure achievements that trains represent. To honor this and also explore my city, I have undertaken the goal of riding every subway line from start to finish.

### Definitions for the pedants

What does it mean to ride every train line from start to finish? What about local vs express variants? What about weekend vs weekday? Late night? And more category questions!

Here's my working definition. It probably can be refined but it's good enough as is for my purposes.

A subway line is defined as the pair of start and end stations, the set and order of intermediary stops, running with a specific bullet designation, up to isomorphism of reversing the exact route. Change any of those components and it's a different line.

Below I enumerate all the different routes as far as I can conclude from that definition based on the [MTA timetables](https://www.mta.info/schedules) published for each "service".
